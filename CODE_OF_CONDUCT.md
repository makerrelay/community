# Code of Conduct

MakerRelay community spaces are for practical, respectful discussion about product feedback, bugs, listings, and public roadmap ideas.

Expected behavior:

- Be specific and constructive.
- Assume public issues may be read by listing owners and other builders.
- Share evidence for listing corrections or abuse reports.
- Keep private information, credentials, and sensitive security details out of public issues.

Unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Spam or repeated low-effort promotion.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Posting malware, exploit instructions, or unsafe links.

MakerRelay may edit, lock, or remove issues that violate these rules.
